About

This is a simulator of the popular arcade rhythm game "maimai".

The goal of this simulator is to make maimai accessible to anyone without any downloads.

  • Slide notes are hard to implement, so they act as tap notes.
  • Working notes: Tap, Break, Hold
  • This is an experimental project, so charts shown here may not reflect the actual chart in the game.

Credits

Notes
